Recommended Reading

Selected bibliography of reviews and syntheses on insect decline and publications related to the main research themes of the RCN: data aggregation and monitoring, quantitative analysis of insect time series, causes of declines, consequences of declines, and solutions.

Monitoring Projects

Interactive map of known long-term insect monitoring projects, including newly initiated efforts that plan to collect data for at least 10 years into the future. Map does not include study sites that are not explicitly designated as monitoring locations (e.g. LTER sites).

Societies

Interactive map of known entomological societies globally, including those whose members are unlikely to work on matters related to insect decline but may have valuable data (e.g. insect pest management societies).
