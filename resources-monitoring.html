Insect Monitoring Programs

If you know of a long-term insect monitoring program that is not currently included in this map, we want to know about it! For this resource, we are only including sites that are specifically monitoring insects (as opposed to long-term ecological research where there is typically some type of experimental manipulation) and that have operated or plan to operate for at least a decade. For other types of long-term insect datasets, see the EntoGEM project. Please email the Program Coordinator, Eliza Grames (egrames@unr.edu), with questions or to add your monitoring site to the directory.
